Macaulay Culkin's girlfriend Brenda Song was spotted for the first time with their baby son Dakota after her secret pregnancy.

The couple welcomed their first child together in early April.

The 33-year-old actress was seen out and about for the first time since giving birth while on a stroll with her mother in Los Angeles.

Brenda cradled Dakota in a blanket and held him close during the walk as she enjoyed quality time with her mother.

She appeared comfortable in a Louis Vuitton bucket hat, an oversized blouse, linen pants and white Golden Goose sneakers.

The Suit Life of Zack and Cody alum gave birth on Monday, April 5, in Los Angeles, with her and Macaulay’s newborn son weighing 6 pounds, 14 ounces.

The new parents revealed the news to Esquire, sharing: “We’re overjoyed.”

The outlet reported that Brenda, who currently stars on Hulu's Dollface, was doing well and is healthy following the birth.

Macaulay, 40, and the Disney Channel alum started dating back in 2017 after they met on the set of their movie Changeland. 

The couple now live together and have three cats – Apples, Dude, and Santa – and a fish named Cinnamon.

In an Esquire cover story last year, the Home Alone star revealed that he and Brenda were ready to have a family by joking that they "practice a lot."

The 40-year-old added: “We’re figuring it out, making the timing work. Because nothing turns you on more than when your lady comes into the room and says, ‘Honey, I’m ovulating.’”

Before his relationship with Brenda, Macaulay famously dated Mila Kunis for five years until 2006.

When he was just 18, the child star married actress Rachel Miner in 1998.

However, the couple separated in 2000 and divorced in 2002.
