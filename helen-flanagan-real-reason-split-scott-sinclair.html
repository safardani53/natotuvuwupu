HELEN Flanagan has revealed the real reason behind her split from fiancé Scott Sinclair.

The Sun told in October last year how Helen, 33, and footballer Scott, 34, had called time on their relationship.

Now actress Helen has spoken out about the heartbreaking decision to walk away from her relationship after 13 years.

In a new TikTok video titled "dating as a single mum", the former Coronation Street star said: "I was with my ex forever.

"I was with my ex from 19 until 31, and he was like my one.

"I remember when we first got together, I was so in love with him. He was the best thing that ever happened to me really because he was my life.
