Kim Kardashian goes goth in black lace gown with gloves for Kourtney's wedding as fans say event loo

 Posted on February 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  659 words  |  Aldo Pusey

KIM Kardashian has gone goth in a black lace gown with gloves for Kourtney Kardashian's Italy wedding.

Some fans said the official event looked more like a "funeral."

Kourtney, 43, and Travis Barker, 46, said their third "I dos" on Sunday, May 22.

The whiplash-inducing wedding weekend comes after their legal marriage at a courthouse in Santa Monica last week and Las Vegas nuptials that were "just for fun" last month.

The couple rented out an entire castle in Portofino for the reception, and the ceremony was held at Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana's seaside compound - as many family members were "on brand," in D&G.

However, as photos of Kim, 41, with her family flowed in - some said it looked like a different ceremony.

Kim wore her blonde hair up with a dramatic long black dress, lace sleeves, gloves and multiple cross necklaces.

She walked with North 8, who earlier wore a CORSET - but matched this time in a black cape, with Reign Disick, 7, in a tuxedo-shorts combo.

As many of the family members seemed to have indeed donned black for the main event, many fans thought one thing:

It looked like a funeral.

'GOTH WEST'

A Reddit thread ensued as one wrote on North's drapery: "Goth West, formerly known as North West."

Another commented, "don't ever let your wedding be sponsored by D&G."

A third wrote: "I think they lied to us…it’s a funeral not a wedding."

As someone, unfortunately, wrote, "North’s outfit reminds me of the [coven of Vampires from Twilight] Volturi lol"

Another harshly replied to that user, "[North] said 'Travis dressed like Voldemort, I wanna be a Deatheater!'"

Someone else wrote: "full Sicilian widow drag" as another concluded, "I’m sorry. But these looks this weekend are a mess."

OMINOUS WEDDING

Kourtney wore traditional white; a short lace Dolce & Gabanna dress with a cathedral veil - but before saying "I do," she shared photos of her pre-wedding attire: indeed a short black dress and a black veil with blue trim, like her sisters.

The rocker had on a black tuxedo as he stood next to his bride-to-be.

Khloe, 37, styled her long blonde hair in waves as she also wore a black dress - she also had a golden crown on top of her head.

Kris, 66, however, was spotted wearing a glittery pink dress and had on fancy silver earrings.

MGK, 31 - who is engaged to Megan Fox, 35 - wore a bright blue tuxedo as he headed on over to the ceremony.

The guests watched as the couple walked down the aisle, the two were holding hands as they knelt down on the floral cushions of their fancy chairs.

Kendall Jenner, 26, and her boyfriend Devin Booker, 25, are there for the weekend as well, as are Kylie Jenner, Stormi, 4, and Travis Scott - the beauty mogul wore black too for the ceremony.

According to PEOPLE, Italian opera tenor Andrea Bocelli will perform at the main event, and Beyonce reportedly flew in too.

After the wedding, the Hulu star will become the stepmother to Travis' teenage kids: Alabama, 16; and Landon, 18.

Kourtney and her ex Scott Disick, 38, are the parents of Mason, 12, Penelope, nine, and Reign.
