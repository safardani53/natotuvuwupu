Who is Tekashi 6ix9ine's girlfriend, Yailin La Ms Viral?

 Posted on October 7, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  979 words  |  Martina Birk

THERE'S never a dull moment in the rapper's music, and that same logic applies to his tumultuous personal life.

Rapper Tekashi 6ix9ine made headlines back in October 2023 when he was arrested in the Dominican Republic for allegedly beating up two music producers working with Yailin La Más Viral.

Who is Yailin La Más Viral?

Jorgina Lulú Guillermo Díaz, known professionally as Yailin La Más Viral, was born on July 4, 2002, in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

Yailin is a rapper, singer, and dembow artist.

Dembow is a Dominican music genre, named after the 1990 song Dem Bow by Jamaican dancehall artist Shabba Ranks.

The genre can be traced to a Jamaican dancehall riddim, or the rhythm section, also known as the instrumental part of the song.

Yailin is known for fusing dembow with other types of music genres, including rap and trap music, along with reggaeton and dancehall.

After signing with Akino Mundial Music in 2019, she released her first song, Quién Me Atraca a Mí, in August 2020.

In 2021, she was featured in El Villano RD's song Chivrika, which went viral on TikTok.

The duo also released a music video to accompany the hit song.

Yailin has collaborated with a number of artists, including Tokischa, La Perversa, Anuel AA, and Tekashi 6ix9ine.

On April 1, 2022, Yailin and Anuel AA released Si Tú Me Busca, produced by Súbelo Neo.

Yailin also appeared on Tekashi 6ix9ine's third studio album, Leyenda Viva, which translates to Living Legend, released on June 16, 2023,

Yailin appears in the song Pa Ti with the American rapper.

When did Tekashi 6ix9ine's and Yailin start dating?

It's not completely clear when and where 6ix9ine and Yailin's relationship officially started, but things started heating up during the summer of 2023.

6ix9ine was previously linked to influencer Rachel Wattley, who goes by the name Jade.

During the 2023 Heat Latin Music Awards, Yailin told the media that she was officially single, confirming that she had separated from Anuel AA.

On June 9, 2023, Yailin released a song, Narcisista, which called out an unnamed ex for not being real.

Just a few days later, Yailin and 6ix9ine posted multiple videos on Instagram, where the rapper showered her with expensive purses and watches.

The duo claimed it wasn't romantic, and that they were posting to promote their new song, Pa Ti.

On June 16, 2023, the song's music video dropped, showcasing the artists' matching rainbow hair and featuring lyrics that focused on not being available for exes.

On Yailin's birthday, 6ix9ine posted a video on his Instagram, showing him gifting the singer a Rolex, packs of hundred dollar bills, flowers, and a cake.

According to Billboard, he captioned the now-deleted post: "In the short time that I have known you I can tell that you are a beautiful person inside and out."

By mid-July, Anuel AA and Tekashi 6ix9ine got into a fight on social media, calling out each other's salacious personal lives.

Before the end of the month, 6ix9ine gifted Yailin with his most lavish purchase yet: a black Rolls-Royce.

Just a few months later, the seemingly happy couple's relationship hit a rough patch when 6ix9ine was arrested in the Dominican Republic.

The rapper, along with four friends, had gone to a music studio where Yailin was reportedly recording new music with producer Diamond La Mafia.

By December 15, 2023, the couple's relationship somehow had gotten even worse, with TMZ reporting that Yailin had been arrested at the rapper's Florida home for assaulting him and his Bentley.

6ix9ine told police the two had been arguing for most of the day, but confirmed that he never got physical with his girlfriend.

Yailin, meanwhile, told the police that her boyfriend had been withholding her passport and her money, which caused the argument.

Time will tell whether the two reconcile, or, like their previous relationships, call it quits.

Who is Yailin La Mas Viral's ex-husband, Anuel AA?

Anuel AA is a Puerto Rican rapper and singer.

Born Emmanuel Gazmey Santiago on November 26, 1992, Anuel AA started recording music when he was just 14 years old.

In the mid-2010s, he signed with the Latin division of Rick Ross's Maybach Music Group.

By 2016, he released his first mixtape, Real Hasta La Muerte.

His success was cut short, however, when he began a 30-month prison sentence that same year for illegally possessing a firearm in Puerto Rico.

Auel AA recorded the entirety of his debut album, Real Hasta La Muerte, while behind bars.

He released the album the day he was released from prison, on July 17, 2018.

The album quickly became a commercial and critical hit.

Since then, the rapper has released three more albums: Emmanuel in 2020, Las Leyendas Nunca Mueren in 2021, and LLNM 2 in 2022.

He also collaborated with Ozuna on a joint album, Los Dioses, in 2021.

Throughout his career, Anuel AA has collaborated with Daddy Yankee, Bad Bunny, Travis Barker, Karol G, Shakira, Lil Wayne, Enrique Iglesias, and J Balvin.

He's even collaborated with Tekashi 6ix9ine, being featured in the rapper's song BEBE.

Unfortunately, the musical collaboration wasn't enough to solidify the duo as friends.

Anuel AA started dating Yailin La Más Viral, Tekashi's current girlfriend, sometime in 2021.

The duo confirmed their relationship on social media in January 2022, and just a few months later, in June 2022, the couple got married.

They announced their separation in February 2023, just one month before their daughter, Cattleya, was born.

It is unclear whether the duo have officially divorced, or remain separated.
