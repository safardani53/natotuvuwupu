NEARLY two years after her split with New York Governor Andrew Cuomo, celebrity chef Sandra Lee has reportedly found herself a new man.

This time around, Lee decided to ditch the lawmakers in suits and hook up with a Hollywood star.

Who is actor Ben Youcef?

Ben Youcef, 42, was born in Algiers on the Mediterranean coast.

He is a nonprofit interfaith leader, as well as a father of two.

Youcef separated from his wife in 2019 before they later filed for divorce in January 2020.

The promising actor was discovered by Steven Spielberg in Munich, Germany.

Youcef was a renowned theater actor who studied in London before working as a top model in New York City for leading designers and advertisers.

After moving to Los Angeles, Youcef appeared on Law & Order, CSINY, NCIS: LA and Chicago PD.

He starred in the 2014 film, The Algerian, based on a story he wrote, and won best actor at the Downtown LA & London International Film Festivals.

What is his net worth?

Youcef's fortune reportedly stands at $850,000, per Net Worth Post.

Is Ben Youcef dating Sandra Lee?

On August 2, Lee and Youcef were spotted dining out at the posh L'Opera restaurant, according to Page Six.

Lee and Cuomo dated for 14 years before splitting amid allegations that he cheated on her, which the governor has denied.

A source told the outlet that Lee feels good at the end of a weight loss journey she's been sharing with fans.

"Between her recent birthday, and feeling great and healthy, Sandra feels especially grateful and blessed for her life right now," the source said.

However, the same can't be said for Cuomo, whose sexual harassment scandal reached new heights on August 3.

A source confirmed to People that Lee and Youcef are officially a couple.

"They became friends when they met at a Santa Monica restaurant in March. It's very early, but they seem to have an undeniable connection," the source told the outlet.

"But both are taking things slowly and enjoying getting to know each other.

"Meeting each other during Covid was challenging. He's become her friend and loving confidante quickly," the source added.
