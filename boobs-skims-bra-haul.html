A FASHION influencer has shared her bra haul from Kim Kardashian's Skims.

The content creator, who wears a bra size 32B, revealed her favorite item from the haul.

TikTok user Alice (@alicehendersonn) shared her "honest review" of four popular Skims bras.

In the clip's caption, the influencer revealed that she purchased each item in the color Mica.

For her first look, the TikToker tried on the $44 Naked scoop bra in a size XS.

Alice said: "This one is so comfortable, the only problem I had with it was the side profile.

"I thought it looked kind of unnatural so I haven’t decided if I’m going to keep this one," she told her followers.

Next, Alice shared her thoughts on the $44 Naked plunge bra, also in a size XS.

She said: "I’ve seen this one everywhere on TikTok, I feel like it’s kind of viral but I just did not like it.

"It’s too thin and not supportive. It gave me no shape and you could see everything so I’m for sure going to return this one because it did nothing for me," Alice added.

The influencer also modeled the $48 Wireless form T-shirt demi bra in a size 32B.

"I love this one, it’s so underrated. I don’t see many people talking about it but it gives you a lot of shape and is so comfortable at the same time so I highly recommend, I’m going to keep it," she said.

Finally, the content creator tried on the $52 Fits Everybody plunge bra, also in size 32B.

"Now this one gave me a lot of support and it looks really good but it was nowhere near as comfortable as the other bras. I’m a comfort girlie so I’m going to probably return this one," Alice said.
