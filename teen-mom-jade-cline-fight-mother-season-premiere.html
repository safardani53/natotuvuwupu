TEEN Mom: The Next Chapter's season premiere has given fans something to talk about as Jade Cline got in a massive fight with her mother.

Wednesday's new episode showed Jade, 26, and her mother having a falling out after Jade picked out her wedding dress.

Jade and her mom, Christy Smith, have had a rocky relationship that has played out throughout the duration of Teen Mom.

While Jade and her crew went dress shopping during last night's episode, Christy and Jade's best friend Briana DeJesus, 29, were present for the exciting event.

Jade's bridesmaids and grandma were also there to help Jade pick out the perfect gown.

While Christy loved the dress her daughter said yes to, she made it very clear that she did not approve of the $10,000 price tag attached.

As Briana, Jade, and her mom got back into the car after the fitting, Jade announced that her feelings were hurt when her mom was yelling inside the store about the hefty price.

From the back seat, Christy then asked her daughter if she was able to get the dress consultant down on the price.

After Jade said the dress actually ended up costing more after tax was added, Christy responded: "I mean it's your money you spend it how you want I just worry about you."

'ALWAYS THE PROBLEM'

Jade then said the ordeal made her cry, to which her mother proceeded to call herself the "f**king problem."

The Teen Mom: The Next Chapter star tried to justify the large expense to her mom, but Christy replied by just telling Jade that she's just "not going to be involved" anymore.

Briana chimed into the argument to defend her friend and emphasize the fact that it was going to be "Jade's special day."

Christy got even more visibly upset and started crying, saying she was going to get out of the car and Uber home instead.

She then ripped the microphone out of her shirt and shoved the MTV camera that was placed in the backseat of the car out of her face.

JADE'S BIG DAY

Jade and her fiance Sean Austin, 29, are set to tie the knot this upcoming fall.

The two share a five-year-old daughter, Kloie.

Recently, Jade shared some details about her upcoming big day.

She posted pictures from a photoshoot on Instagram, revealing in her caption: "I'll be Mrs. Austin October 6, 2023. Can't wait to share this journey with you guys!"

Jade shared that she will be having roughly 200 guests, her daughter Kloie will be the flower girl and they're heading to Turks and Caicos for their honeymoon.

She also told fans that they plan to invite most of the Teen Mom cast to the big day, but they didn't name any names.

When one follower asked: "Will your wedding be on MTV?" she responded: "You'll have to watch to see."

Ahead of the on-screen proposal, fans had been speculating that Jade and Sean were secretly married.

Some fans have also been speculating that Jade and Austin may be expecting their second baby.

BABY ON BOARD?

Recently, some fans have noticed an unusual trend in her Instagram photos.

One eagle-eyed fan took to a popular Teen Mom forum to share their thoughts, writing: "Call me crazy but I think Jade is pregnant. They got wedding pics done already. I haven't seen many belly pics, in fact, seems like she's hiding her belly maybe? Lol"

The user was referring to posts shared by Jade, including one she posted in May of her and Sean dressed in bride and groom attire. 

She captioned it: "Only 4 months and 16 days left till we say 'I Do,'" along with the hashtag "#Mrs.Austin."

Despite Jade stating her upcoming wedding date, many of her followers found it odd that she already took wedding photos and suspected a pregnancy might be the reason.
