Vanna White reveals real style away from Wheel of Fortune in rare casual look without makeup

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  520 words  |  Aldo Pusey

VANNA White has shown off her real, natural look away from Wheel of Fortune with a no-makeup clip.

The 66-year-old took to Instagram with a quick video promoting an "essential" part of her makeup routine.

Standing in front of the Wheel letter board, Vanna applied the Laura Geller Beauty Serum Blush Cheek Tint to show fans what it did to her face.

She captioned the video: "What is an essential part of my makeup routine? Blush! It makes such a difference to brighten up my face! My @lauragellerbeauty Serum Blush Cheek Tint does the trick every single time."

Vanna continued: "Whether I’m going out on the town, turning letters on Wheel of Fortune, or having a casual night with friends and family, I love wearing my Serum Blush Cheek Tint - it’s so light and refreshing. I am wearing the shade Practical Pink in this video."

Vanna finishes off her look by curling her eyelashes.
