Who is Michael Symon's wife?

 Posted on May 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  361 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

OVER the years, celebrity chef Michael Symon has been seen on several Food Network shows.

When he is not in the kitchen, he can often be found at home, spending time with his wife.

Who is Michael Symon's wife?

Michael's career began in the early 1990s after he got a job at a 40-seat restaurant called Players.

His wife, Liz Sanderson Symon, also worked at that restaurant and managed the front of the house.

"I was impressed by the way she carried herself. She was very pretty and has a presence," Michael told The New York Times in 2018.

After meeting at the restaurant, the pair formed a friendship that later became something more after he got her a job at a new restaurant a few years later.

“We’d get done with work, the staff would go out together, we’d get drinks, then we’d make out and think, ‘That was a bad idea, we should just be friends,’” Michael continued.

“Then a week would pass, we’d go out again, and the same thing would happen.” 

After two years of dating, he then proposed and they tied the knot on November 1, 1998.

Do Michael and Liz have kids?

Since getting married, Michael and Liz have dominated the culinary industry by opening up several successful restaurants.

While Michael might not have any children of his own, he is the stepfather of Liz's son Kyle, who she welcomed during her first marriage.

At the beginning of their relationship, Kyle was 6 years old. He is now all grown up and has two children of his own with his wife, Krista.

What is Michael Symon's net worth?

Over the years, Michael has been able to amass a fortune due to the success of his culinary career.

As of November 2022, his net worth is an estimated $6million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
