KOURTNEY Kardashian has shown off her $500 daily supplement routine in new pictures after fans slam her for "flaunting wealth."

Kourtney, 43, has taken to her Instagram stories to post a shot of the multiple supplements that she consumes as a part of her supplement "lineup."

In the photo there were few different vitamins that were placed on a table alongside her nettle leaf tea, roasted veggie salad and quail eggs.

Nestled near her tea were two matcha gummies from her band Lemme, priced at $55, Liposomal NAD + Platinum, priced at $80, and a blurred supplement titled "lunchtime supplement."

Following her story outlining a few of the supplements that she takes, the Hulu star posted a story promoting her website Poosh's latest article that detailed her supplement routine.

The lowest costing supplement that the mother-of-three takes, Berbercap, was priced at $25, while the most expensive one, OvaVite Preconception Prenatal Vitamins, was priced at $155.

WHAT ITS FOR

Alongside the article highlighting the price of each supplement that the Poosh founder uses, it also explained what she utilizes each one for.

Kourtney uses her most expensive supplement OvaVite because it's a prenatal capsule that helps her body prepare for making a baby since she wants to conceive.

In the morning the eldest Kardashian sibling takes the $58 supplement Methylfolate Supplement.

At no specified time she consumes Strawberry Prenatal DHA, priced at $48, R-Lipoic Acid, priced at $44, and the Liposomal NAD+ Platinum that was referenced in her story.

RUBBING IT IN

Kourtney coming out with her expensive supplement routine validated fans' believe that she flaunts her wealth.

Back in September Kourtney was slammed by fans for inadvertently bragging about her finances after she shared to her Instagram story a vegan buffet prepared by a costly private chef.

The meal included lettuce wraps, rolls with butter, a type of baked pastry, rice, a corn and vegetable salad, and other vegetables.

The vegan meal also included a plate of food that gave the appearance of pulled pork or other meat.

In the Instagram posts, Kourtney said the meal was for "Sunday night family dinner." The meal was "all vegan and mostly gluten-free."

Observers on Reddit were intrigued by Kourtney's elaborate vegan meal.
