ALL Crocs lovers know how vital the Jibbitz can be.

The little charms that attach to the holes on the front of the shoes can transform the footwear into something eye-catching and colourful.

But a new range of the charms has left people disgusted.

A video was shared on TikTok by a silicone prop company, who have designed the inserts to make it look as though your big toe is peeping through one of the holes.

The person modelling the shoes also lifted up their foot and wiggled it - to show how the realistic charms wobbled when they did so.

However, people in the comments section admitted they'd been doing double takes at the additions.
