Jessica Simpson Puts Her Assets Front and Center in Sheer Shirt

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  192 words  |  Martina Birk

Oh, hey there! Putting her assets front and center, Jessica Simpson entered LAX on Friday, Nov. 6, wearing a fitted all-black ensemble, including a semi-sheer blouse.

The Jessica Simpson Collection CEO, 35, slipped the see-through top over a lacy black bra, teamed with skinny jeans, Christian Louboutin peep-toes, and a color-blocked handbag. She finished the ensemble with voluminous curls and a light raspberry lip.

Meanwhile, her husband, Eric Johnson, with whom she shares children Maxwell, 3, and Ace, 2, kept one hand on her back while modeling a slate T-shirt, brown pants, and black kicks.

The week before, the couple slayed the Halloween scene in an epic duo costume. Inspired by the 1983 classic National Lampoon’s Vacation, Johnson dressed as Chevy Chase‘s Clark Griswold, while Simpson was the perfect Christie Brinkley, who starred as the Girl in the Ferrari. 

Brinkley was so excited to see the pair’s getups, she even Instagramed about it. “How cute is this?” she wrote, re-gramming one of Simpson’s snaps. “Our National Lampoon was lampooned to PERFECTION! You guys look Hot and Hilarious!” Simpson soon replied back, “OMG @christiebrinkley you just made my husband cream his jeans!”
