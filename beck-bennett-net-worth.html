Who is Beck Bennett?

Beck Bennett is an American actor, comedian, and writer best known for his work on Saturday Night Live.

Bennett is leaving SNL after writing and starring on the show for the past eight seasons.

Bennett took to Instagram today to announce his departure.

"Love you, SNL, Gonna miss you so much. Thank you for 8 years of remarkable people and incredible experiences that completely changed my life. I had so much fun," he wrote.

Who is Beck Bennett's wife Jessy Hodges?

Born in August of 1986, the 35-year-old is best known for her acting role in web series Anything Like Me.

Hodges also appeared in shows like PEN15, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, and Indebted.

Born to Ellen Sandweiss, who starred in 1981 hit The Evil Dead, acting is in Hodges' blood. She also is listed as a producer for shorts Sundowners and Games for Girls.

Hodges received a degree in Drama at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts.

Also known for her public political endorsements, Hodges has taken to social media to support the Biden/Harris administration.

How long have they been married?

The couple kept their relationship private for years, beginning to date in 2011 but not taking the relationship public until 2016.
