Yolanda Foster Posts Bikini Photo From Tahiti, Future With 'Housewives' Uncertain

 Posted on May 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  362 words  |  Martina Birk

If you haven’t already felt vacation or bathing suit envy, then this should do the trick.

Yolanda Foster posted a gorgeous photo of herself, clad in a black two-piece and scrubbing sand all over her body in Tahiti.

"Body Scrub, Body Care………. #NaturalHealing #Tahiti #Summer," she wrote.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star is taking a much-needed holiday for herself, and the Instagrams are making us want to embrace the monster that is Monday morning and power through the rest of the week.

The Dutch model wrote on a photo showing her posing next to a palm tree.

"Looking into a happy & healthy future, never looking back……… #IslandLife #Intentions #MagicMorning."

"You have to fight through some Bad Days to earn the Best Days of your life……. #Solitude #Happiness #Gratitude," another one read, this time featuring a photo of Foster in a white bikini and an ocean-soaked top.

Foster took the trip during a crucial time for the Beverly Hills franchise.

Casting calls are reportedly being made and executives are trying to determine who will be asked back for next season, and who is shown the door.

Reality site Tamara Tattles reports:

"Kathryn [Edwards] was not given a contract. Eileen [Davidson] is being asked to be a FOH [friend of housewives] with Rinna but bawking at her terms, Yolanda is OUT!" 

Us Weekly also claims that Edwards was fired from Beverly Hills because she "just wasn’t entertaining enough for viewers."

Edwards could possibly be replace by reality hopeful Sydney Holland,ex-girlfriend of studio executive, Sumner Redstone, 93.  

Holland reportedly approached producers with a reel and full proposal on why she would make an excellent addition to the cast.

Foster is also in the middle of divorce proceedings with estranged husband, David Foster.  The couple announced their split on December 1st, 2015.

Though they signed a pre-nuptial agreement before their 2011 wedding, the Canadian music producer has agreed to give her more than what was originally agreed upon.

"Yolanda isn’t getting enough to retire," a source told TMZ, but added that David was "generous."
