A HAIRSTYLIST has revealed how she was able to transform her fine tresses using a serum that dramatically boosts volume.

She admitted to having previously been skeptical of beauty products that promise to make fine hair strands thicker.

Nicole Pomme (@nicolepommehair) has almost 11,000 followers on TikTok, where she shares advice for treating different hair types.

She took to the social media platform raving about an effective product for improving hair fullness.

She said there isn’t a magic product that instantly makes fine strands thicker, but revealed a serum that has worked wonders for her over time. 

“I have the finest hair ever,” Nicole said. “I’ve also got a lot of clients with super fine hair.
