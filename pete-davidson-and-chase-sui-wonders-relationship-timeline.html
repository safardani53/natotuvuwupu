Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders Relationship Timeline

 Posted on January 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  157 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders continued to grow closer after filming their movie Bodies Bodies Bodies — and their relationship turned romantic in 2023.

The pair met in 2021 while working on the dark comedy, which was released the following year. Rumors of a potential romance between Davidson and Wonders started to swirl in December 2022 when the twosome were seen attending a New York Rangers game at Madison Square Garden with Rachel Sennott. At the time, Davidson was linked to Emily Ratajkowski, but their romance fizzled out before the new year.

Following his split from Ratajkowski, Davidson started spending more time with Wonders. In January 2023, the actors were spotted together on several outings — including a lunch date in New York City and a Hawaiian vacation.

Us confirmed in August 2023 that Davidson and Wonders called it quits.
