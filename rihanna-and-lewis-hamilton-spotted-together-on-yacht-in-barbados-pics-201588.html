Rihanna and Lewis Hamilton Spotted Together on Yacht in Barbados: Pics

 Posted on June 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  224 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Just livin’ that island life. Rihanna and Formula One race car champion Lewis Hamilton were spotted hanging out together on a yacht in Barbados on Friday, Aug. 7, keeping fans guessing as to whether the pair are an item or not.

The “Stay” singer and Hamilton, 30, soaked in the sun in her native Barbados, and even spent some time aboard small rafts that took off from the yacht. Rihanna, 27, wore a colorful, skimpy bikini, and Hamilton donned a white tank top and a backwards baseball cap.

The duo first sparked rumors of a potential romance after they were photographed together at Barbados’ annual Grand Kadooment Day carnival, where she performed. 

Neither Hamilton nor the singer have commented on their are-they-aren’t-they relationship.

Hamilton has been linked to other A-listers since splitting from longtime girlfriend Nicole Scherzinger in February, including Kendall Jenner. The athlete and Jenner, 19, reportedly hit it off while hanging out at a star-studded amfAR 22nd Cinema Against AIDS Gala event in Cannes, France, in May.
