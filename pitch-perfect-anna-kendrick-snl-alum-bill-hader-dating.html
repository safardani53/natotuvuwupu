Pitch Perfect star Anna Kendrick &amp; SNL alum Bill Hader have been 'secretly dating' for over a year

 Posted on March 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  419 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

PITCH Perfect star Kendrick and Saturday Night Live alum Bill Hader have been "secretly dating" for over and year.

The actress is "really happy" as the couple "keep each other laughing."

PEOPLE reported on Thursday that Anna, 36, and Bill, 43, have been dating for over a year.

A source told the outlet: "Anna has been dating Bill quietly for over a year.

"They met years ago. She's hosted Saturday Night Live and they've done a movie together, but they got together well after the movie."

The couple previously starred in the Disney+ film Noelle, which premiered on the streaming service in 2019.

The insider continued: "They are both very private people, and with the pandemic it was easy to keep it quiet.

"They're both hysterical so they must keep each other laughing all of the time. She's really, really happy."

Reps for both Anna and Bill refused to comment on their relationship when contacted by PEOPLE.

PAST ROMANCES

Before dating Anna, Bill was in a relationship with actress Rachel Bilson, 40, for six months.

The former couple, who broke up in July 2020, starred together in the 2013 romantic comedy The To Do List.

Prior to his romance with The O.C. actress, Bill was married to director Maggie Carey.

Maggie, who happened to direct The To Do List, married Bill in 2006 and they divorced in 2018.

The former couple share three daughters: Hayley Clementine, seven, Harper, nine, and Hannah Kathryn, 12.

ANNA'S LOVE LIFE

Meanwhile, Anna has mostly kept her romantic life out of the spotlight.

She dated English filmmaker Edgar Wright from 2009 until 2013.

The Twilight actress also began a relationship with cinematographer Ben Richardson in February 2014, though the romance didn't last.

Despite keeping her past relationships on the down low, Anna previously opened up about her dating history while promoting her HBO Max series Love Life. 

During a 2020 interview with The Sydney Morning Herald, Anna said she's "just so happy that I'm clearer now about what I will accept from people in my life."
