Faith Hill and Tim McGraw Rock Red Carpet PDA, Look Like Newlyweds at Oscars

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  2 minutes  |  259 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Tim McGraw and Faith Hill walked the red carpet of the 2015 Academy Awards like a pair of handsy newlyweds, though the couple has been married for almost 20 years.

Tim McGraw and Faith Hill divorce rumors have been flying around for years. But you wouldn’t know it by the way the couple were acting at last night’s Oscars!

Faith laughed every time McGraw tried to cinch his hands a little tighter around her waist.

They seemed more like a high school couple posing for prom pictures than two people who’ve been married for almost 20 years.

Tim was there to sing the Oscar nominated ballad, I’m Not Going to Miss You, from the documentary, Glen Campbell: I’ll Be Me. Faith was there to look beautiful and support her man.

The couple was playful, posing and giggling with photographers all night. Faith was sporting her pixie-style hair cut, while Tim WASN’T sporting his trusty cowboy hat for the first time since … well it’s been a while with that thing.

John Legend and Common’s Glory won the Oscar for best song last night, but Faith still beamed with pride as her hubby took the stage and sang the soulful track from the only documentary nominated in the category.
