Leland Tyler Wayne, professionally known as Metro Boomin, is an American Record Producer, Recording Director, Musician and DJ led by SAL & CO. Growing up in St. Louis, Wayne started his career as a producer in high school and became famous for his successful recordings with Atlanta's hip hop and trap artists such as Future, 21 Savage, Gucci Mane, and Migos. in the mid-2010s. In 2017, Forbes called him "easily one of the world's most in-demand hit makers", while Stereogum described him as "one of rap music's most original, vivid and important voices. today ". Wayne's early production success came with hits like ILoveMakonnen's 2014 hit “Tuesday” and Drake and Future's 2015 single “Jumpman”. Since then he has amassed over 20 hits, including “Bad and Boujee "by Migos," Mask Off "by Future," Bank Account "by 21 Savage," Congratulations "by Post. Malone, "Tunnel Vision" by Kodak Black and "Heartless" by The Weeknd.
