Sounds great, right? However, you might be unsure about how it all works.

DealDash offers a sustainable alternative to burning old inventory, which has been reported that well known brands practice, rather than selling at a reduced rate.

Instead, DealDash purchases the excess stock and offers it to savvy shoppers through its auctions.

In addition to helping companies move large batches of surplus inventory, DealDash partners with well-known brands, major wholesalers and top retailers to sell items via auctions - alongside their traditional sales channels. 

This relationship means DealDash is able to receive promotional discounts on stock it acquires that can then be passed onto bargain hunters.

Despite the stock being “excess inventory”, all products sold on DealDash are brand new and premium quality. Shipping is always free and there's also a 90-day money-back guarantee on your first bid purchase, so a first-time try is completely risk-free.

How can I get started bidding on DealDash?

It’s really easy to get started with bidding on the items you want on DealDash.

Each bid costs between 12¢ to 15¢ and all the auctions start at $0.00, so once you buy a pack of bids you’ll be ready to start.

As soon as someone places the first bid in an auction, the price goes up by a penny and a 10-second timer will begin.

If no one else bids on the item during the timer, then the last bidder wins, walking away with a coveted item at a fraction of the buy-it-now price or RRP.

You can also opt to exchange your winning item for more bids, which can be used in any future auctions you decide to take part in.

If you don’t win the auction, you can choose to purchase through the buy-it-now option instead, and claim back any bids you used in the auction.
