See Photos Of All Her Hosting Outfits Hollywood Life

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  732 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Taraji P. Henson turned the American Music Awards into her very own fashion show on Sunday night! The Golden Globe-winning actress, 50, who is tonight’s emcee, hit the (virtual) red carpet in a stunning silver beaded dress with intricate beading and silver hardware. Taraji accessorized with Tiffany & Co. diamond earrings and pink sapphire and green tourmaline diamond rings. The Proud Mary star, who stepped out in open-toe metallic stilettos, also wore a beaded head piece. — This marks just one of many looks Taraji has in store for the night.

The actress kicked off her hosting gig with an electrifying dance routine to a number of old and new hits. She showed off her dance skills in a white dress and sparkling tights with white sneakers to JAY Z‘s “Dirt Off Your Shoulder,” before she twerked to Megan Thee Stallion‘s “Savage.” Taraji ended her opening performance with Cardi B and Megan’s No. 1 hit, “WAP,” before she put the kibosh on it because she joked, “This is family friendly TV!”

Since there was “no time” for an outfit change, Taraji threw a beige, transparent coat over her matching bedazzled jumpsuit. “I am so happy to be here,” she began, while out of breath from busting a move (or 20). “I’m just happy to be off the couch and in some real fashion,” Taraji admitted in reference to the COVID-19 pandemic. But, she promised that “being at home with Taraji” is going to a “party!”

“It’s time to remember that we are one country y’all and we need to love on each other more. We’re going to have fun, embracing our neighbors and coming together through music,” she said, later adding, “Me and [Jennifer Lopez] are making 50 the new 20!” And, we conquer!

For Taraji’s second look, the What Men Want actress sizzled in another sparkling number. This time, she donned a ruched, halter dress that was a stunning shade of blue. Taraji took it up a notch with her third look — a one-shoulder, cut-out black bodysuit with a transparent slit skirt.

Look No. 4 took viewers back in time when Taraji stepped out in a pink sparkling flapper dress and floppy hat. Her tank top dress had an illustration of keyboard on its right side. Taraji paired the modern throwback with a black thigh high boots.

For her fifth outfit of the night, Taraji channeled Diana Ross with a stunning, voluminous hairstyle, similar to the singer’s iconic locks. As for her tight, thin-strap gown? — Taraji took a page out of her girl Meg Thee Stallion’s book. She rocked a zebra print dress, similar to the rapper’s zebra print corset she wore in the “WAP” music video.

Taraji’s sixth AMAs look gave off major Cookie from Empire vibes. She sizzled in a beige leather skirt and corset top with a mini blazer jacket. The host kept her same hairstyle, a gorgeous afro, for looks No. 6 and 7. Taraji’s seventh ensemble was a curve-hugging lavender dress with a string top that included a silver circle.

Her 8th look of the night featured a metallic silver dress (a big AMAs trend) with puffy sleeves. Taraji’s next ensemble, her ninth (and our favorite!), was a neon yellow beaded dress with one shoulder and a sultry thigh high slit. Additionally, the actress donned Roberto Coin for Mayors Jewelry for three different looks.

This year’s AMAs is being live broadcast on ABC from the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles. JLo, The Weeknd, Billie Eilish, Justin Bieber and more top artists will take the stage for anticipated performances. While the AMAs will look different this time around, Taraji is focused on uplifting the mood after a challenging year.

“The AMAs bring together the music community to celebrate what really drives all artists – the fans,” the Hidden Figures actress said in a press release. “I’m proud to take the stage as this year’s host – and also a fan – for what promises to be an electrifying night of music.”

Tonight’s show marks Taraji’s first public appearance since she confirmed her split from fiancé Kelvin Hayden on the October 19 episode of The Breakfast Club. While discussing the ways women deal with “trauma-filled men,” Taraji said that the longtime relationship just “didn’t work.” 

The Super Bowl XLI champion proposed to Taraji on Mother’s Day in May 2018. The actress and former NFL player were initially scheduled to tie the knot in April, however, she decided to delay the nuptials over potential inclement weather.
