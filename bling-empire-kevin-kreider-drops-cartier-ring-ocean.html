BLING Empire star Kevin Kreider dropped a luxury Cartier ring in the ocean – just minutes after getting the pricey gift from co-star Christine Chiu.

Cast-member Kane Lim shared a series of Instagram Stories on Monday and joked about Kevin's ring mishap during their yacht party.

Kane noted in the clip how Kevin "dropped his Cartier into the ocean" and he could be heard laughing in the video as he yelled "Kevin!" reported Asia One.

Meanwhile, Kevin appeared to be upset and frustrated as his ring sank and said: "That was the only Cartier I got."

The cast were celebrating on the luxurious yacht when Kevin lost his bling, which was a gift from Christine,

In fact, she gave everyone some Cartier jewelry, according to Kane, who remarked: "You get one. Everyone gets Cartier."

Chèrie Chan Siu-Ha also referenced Kevin's ring which is lost at sea in a hilarious Instagram post.

"Sunday Funday with love #BlingEmpire family," she captioned a video of them cheering.

"So grateful for them. We laugh, we cry together. At the end of the day they’ve become my family and we are destined to be friends.

"We all tested negative [for coronavirus] before seeing each other. And some of us haven’t seen each other since 2019," Chèrie added. "[Kevin] donated some priceless jewelry to some lucky dolphins today."

Poor Kevin also referenced the incident on his Instagram Stories noting that "word [got] out fast" about his bling overboard

"Lucky dolphins have my Cartier ring," the Bling Empire hunk captioned a video of the mammals swimming under the yacht.
