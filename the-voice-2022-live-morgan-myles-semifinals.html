Adam Levine’s departure from the show

In short, Adam Levine left the show because he wanted more time with his kids.

“I do miss it, but I also don’t miss how much I had to work,” he said in an episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show.

“I was just constantly working for so many years. Very lucky, very fortunate, very blessed, and all that, but just to be able to stop in this moment to spend time with my new young family and just have the greatest time ever,” he continued.

Kelly Clarkson’s departure from the show

Like Adam Levine, one of Kelly Clarkson’s reason for leaving the show is because of her kids.

“I’ve decided this year there’s just (going to) be a couple of changes for me that I can’t say here,” she said, as reported by Yahoo. 

“I’ve been able to make more time just for me and my kiddos and still be able to work. Just where we can get away on weekends and really do some fun stuff with my kids,” she said.

Carson gives Blake feedback on his music

Carson Daly and Blake Shelton feel so confident in their friendship that Carson isn’t afraid to give his two cents on Blake’s music.

“Here’s how you know that Carson is one of my best friends,” Blake said during an appearance on Today in 2021.

“He texted me when Come Back As A Country Boy came out, he was like, ‘Man, this is seriously, this song is killer, this is my favorite song you’ve ever put out.

“And then flash-forward to about two weeks ago, because I knew he liked the song, I said, ‘Hey, what do you think about the video that just come out?’ He goes, ‘Yeah, I don’t really like your video for that song."

The Voice host officiated judges’ wedding

The Voice saw Blake get close with Gwen Stefani when she joined as a judge in 2014.

They began dating a year later, and in 2021, decided to tie the knot.

Blake asked Carson to officiate the music stars’ matrimony.

“It was incredible to be part of such an important moment in their lives,” Carson said on Today at the time.

The former MTV host asked the couple to write their own vows for the super-secret ceremony at Blake’s ranch in Tishomingo, Oklahoma.

Carson’s response to Blake’s exit

Blake Shelton recently announced that he is leaving the show after series 23, which will begin next March.

Carson has shared his disappointment at waving goodbye to his longtime on-screen pal.

“I hate it,” the host told Entertainment Tonight in October.

“He and I obviously developed a great friendship at The Voice. I’m bummed, but I get it.”

Inside Carson Daly and Blake Shelton’s bromance

It’s no secret that Today anchor Carson, 49, and country singer Blake, 46, are close pals.

Carson and Blake have worked together on The Voice since the show started in 2011, with the former serving as host, and the later serving as a judge.

The two have formed a tight bond across 22 seasons, and they have been the only constant personalities on the show over the last 11 years.
