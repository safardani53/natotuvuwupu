Kim Kardashian has once again been caught on tape with Ray J.

This time, however, the reality star is not being bent over and slammed from behind by her former boyfriend in a tape that would go on to earn both parties millions of dollars.

Instead, Kim is jiggling her breasts for the camera… showing off a penis-shaped bong on which she sucks… and then taking a hit of what we presume to be marijuana from her then-lover’s mouth.

For real.

This is all on tape.

Radar Online has managed to obtain footage of Kardashian and Ray J from some period during their courtship.

The hip hop artist and the beauty maven dated from 2003 through 2006, and their sex tape (Kim Kardashian Superstar) went viral in 2007.

The raunchy amateur porn was filmed in October 2003, however, during a trip to Cabo San Lucas — and it appears as if the opening scene in this video was shot during this same trip.

We cannot say for certain when other scenes were shot.

But we can say a few other things:

1. At one point, Ray J places the aforementioned penis-shaped bong between his legs and Kim sucks on it.

2. At another point, Ray J blows some smoke into the camera and then asks "Kimberly" if she wants a "hit."

3. Kardashian then takes said hit out of Ray J’s mouth and asks for another, but her request is denied.

4. You can visit Radar right now to view the video in full.

We’ve posted screen shots from the tape above and below.

The Kim Kardashian sex tape is, hilariously, back in the news because Kim herself brought it up.

On the most recently episode of her family’s reality show, Kim claimed that she was high on ecstasy at the time she rode Ray J raw on camera.

"I did ecstasy once and I got married. I did it again, I made a sex tape. Like, everything bad would happen," Kim told Scott Disick during this installment.

She added that "everyone" knew this was the case because Kim’s "jaw was shaking" during the red hot action.

This is one of the greatest quotes in human history for a couple reasons.

First, no one was looking at your jaw while viewing this video, Kim. Hate to break it to you.

Second, we’re pretty sure that jaw-shaking is not a well-known side effect of taking ecstasy.

Third, a source close to Ray responded to Kardashian’s claim here by offering up an alternative for why her jaw may have been moving around in the middle of the tape.

Simply put, this insider has said, "her lip was quivering because she was getting f-cked."

There’s no doubt that the footage obtained by Radar is legitimate.

It was filmed several years ago and Kim admitted in this same conversation with Disick that she went through a "wild phase" back in the day.

So none of this is especially revelatory or anything.

But it’s still jarring to watch and to remember that Kim Kardashian was not always this polished, personality-free, money-generating robot.

She once knew how to have fun and she once got a little crazy!

We miss those sides of her.
