Teen Mom Rachel Beaver earns just $450 per episode for first season amid pregnancy drama The US

 Posted on March 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  303 words  |  Aldo Pusey

TEEN Mom: Young and Pregnant star Rachel Beaver earned just $450 per episode for her appearance on the MTV series.

The Sun can exclusively reveal Rachel, 17, is earning $13,500 for season 2.

A source told The Sun that Rachel, who is mom to toddler daughter Hazlee, “isn’t working” and “doesn’t plan on going to school.”

The insider continued: “She is planning to move back to Atlanta when she receives her final check for $4,500.

“I don’t know how she thinks she’s going to pay rent and afford food for her baby and herself.”

As for Rachel's mom Stephanie, who makes frequent appearances on Young and Pregnant, she earned $1,200 for the season.

The source added that the conditions of their Tennessee trailer are “terrible," as there are "roaches everywhere.”

In a video Rachel posted around Christmas, what appeared to be a cockroach crawled out of a present her young daughter unwrapped.

Scenes from inside the trailer she shares with her sister Malorie, 20, and mom Stephanie, 40, show a torn and dirty couch, and clutter.

Rachel’s salary is shocking compared to the stars of other franchises.

RadarOnline reported Jenelle Evans, who was fired in May 2019, earned over $400,000 in 2018 alone for her stint on Teen Mom 2.

MTV has not announced if Young and Pregnant has been renewed for another season.

On this week’s episode, Rachel learned she is pregnant with her second child.

Despite considering abortion, she decided to keep her baby and raise the new addition with Koty, her boyfriend of only a few weeks.

But on next week’s episode, Rachel revealed she is “single again” after a blowout fight with Koty.

MTV did not respond for The Sun's request for comment.

Teen Mom: Young and Pregnant airs Tuesdays at 9/8c on MTV.
