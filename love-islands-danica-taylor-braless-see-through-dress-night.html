Love Island's Danica Taylor wows as she goes braless in see-through dress on night out

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  346 words  |  Martina Birk

LOVE Island's Danica Taylor looked stunning in a daring mesh dress whilst living it up in Vegas.

The star, who rose to fame on season 8 of Love Island, flaunted her glamorous night out on Instagram.

Danica, 22, could be seen snapping a sultry selfie on her stories which she shared to her 654K followers.

Putting on a busty display, the professional dancer rocked a black mini-dress that boasted sheer panels and a figure-hugging silhouette.

The picture of elegance, Danica paired the eye-popping ensemble with minimalistic jewellery and a black clutch-bag.

The star appears to be having a fantastic time in the USA, whilst staying at the Venetian resort in Las Vegas, frequently sharing snippets of her getaway to social media.

She even apologised to her followers for the volume of posts she has been sharing, quipping: "Sorry for spam," before joking, "then we somehow made it out," after a day of shenanigans.

Danica and friends have already enjoyed their fair share of sun and pool-party fun, with the reality TV beauty sporting a gold bikini whilst sipping on a drink pool-side.

The star wasn't afraid of showing off her renowned moves, which she often shared whilst gracing our TV screens, strutting her stuff whilst pool-side.

Danica even showed off her flexibility by craning herself backwards before squatting into a suggestive pose.

The fun didn't stop there, with the girls also heading out to Asian Bistro and esteemed celebrity haunt TAO, where they enjoyed delicious looking food.

Loved by A-listers, the fine-dining spot and nightclub has been frequented by the likes of Madonna, Rihanna, Kim Kardashian and Drake.

Danica also showed the after-math of her glittering night-out, filming herself singing to rock band Def Leppard with the caption: "Why I had this much energy after a whole night out 8 hours behind," followed by rolling-eye emojis.

Danica and pals also headed off to the Lovers and Friends R&B festival, where they watched rapper 50 cent perform.

Missy Elliott, Christina Aguilera, Pitbull, Usher and Mariah Carey are also amongst the show's headliners.
