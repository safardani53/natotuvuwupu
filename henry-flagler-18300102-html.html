Henry Morrison Flagler is an American industrialist and founder of Standard Oil, the first Ohio-based company. He was also a key figure in the development of Florida's Atlantic coast and the founder of the Florida East Coast Railway, the majority of which was built through the hiring of delinquents. He is known as the father of Miami and Palm Beach, Florida.
