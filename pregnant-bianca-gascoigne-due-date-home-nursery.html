Pregnant Bianca Gascoigne reveals her due date as she finishes off nursery at home

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  340 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

BIANCA Gascoigne is eagerly awaiting the arrival of her first child and has confirmed she's just weeks away from giving birth.

The Celebrity Big Brother star is busy prepping her home for the new tot's arrival, but found some time for a little self-care and got her hair done this weekend.

Later sharing her look (and bump in an olive green bodyform dress), Bianca shared the days were getting closer.

"Bun almost done!!" Bianca shared. "In the weeks now beautiful people. What does everyone think of my new hair and colour? I wanted to have a more natural look even thou it's obviously far from natural."

Proud brother Regan Gascoigne was among those celebrating the star as he wrote: "Most Beautiful Sister Ever."

Dancing On Ice star Regan has been proving a helping hand for Bianca as she preps for the baby's arrival, with the star putting his DIY skills to the test to decorate the bedroom.

"Best brother ever. Helping me put some clouds up in the bubbas room love you," she wrote on Instagram, sharing pictures of Regan in a grey baby room filled with white furniture.

The loving sister added: "She is going to be obsessed with you and love you sooo much."

Bianca announced her pregnancy in August alongside boyfriend Arron Wright in hilarious fashion – by recreating the poster for movie Knocked Up. 

In the pic, they were sat in bed surrounded by baby clothes and products as Bianca munched on pizza.

As they've prepared for their new arrival, the party-loving duo went all out with a gender reveal bash dubbed “Glittertot” in honour of Ibiza nightclub “Glitterbox”. 

She’s also been preparing the baby’s bedroom - and teasing the baby girl’s potential name. 

When Bianca was asked by a fan: "Have you thought of any names yet for the baby?"

She replied: "Sure have ... we decided on her name as soon as I found out I was pregnant!

"It begins with a 'B' so she can be Little B and I can be Big B."
