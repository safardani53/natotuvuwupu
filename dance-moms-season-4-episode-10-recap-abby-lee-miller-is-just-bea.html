Abby Lee Miller is Just BEASTING on Fools

 Posted on April 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  317 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 10 showed that Abby Lee Miller is the one in charge. Which we realize sounds awfully obvious, but even by her standards.

The Beast of Lifetime exerted even more power than usual this week. How?

Watch Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 10 Online

At the pyramid meeting, Abby’s satisfaction with the group’s undefeated record is quickly interrupted after Holly relays that she has published a book.

Placing Kalani at the top will hush the rest of ’em up real fast.

Maggie gets her solo and Kendall also gets a lyrical solo, with Kira soon wondering why Kalani can’t go up against Maddie. Abby dismisses that idea.

As for Holly’s book, Abby dismisses the work as a "pamphlet."

Holly’s husband, for his part, shoots her a look of death.

 

The next day, Abby Lee Miller goes off on Kendall (a.k.a. "the new Paige") after Jill complains, calls her out as delusional and threatens to boot Kendall off the squad.

She’s just getting started, too.

Later in Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 10, neither Kendall nor Maddie excel in solos, and backstage, an audition mom claims her daughter was better.

The team does finish first overall and Maddie wins for her individual performance, but all is not well. In fact, it becomes a giant clusterf–k in the green room!

The long and short of it? Abby informs Jill she owes her $500 for Kendall not winning the solo and tells Kalani she’s pissed she didn’t win the improv dance.

Abby yells at Chloe about repeating steps and tells Christi she has an attitude, but Christi gets all up in Abby’s grill and says she isn’t intimidated by her.

Oh it’s ON now!
