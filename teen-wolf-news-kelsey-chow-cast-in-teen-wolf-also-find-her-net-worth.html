Who is Kesley Chow?

Kesley Asbille Chow an American actress was born on September 9, 1991, in Colombia, South Carolina to a Chinese father and mother of English and Cherokee descent. She is the eldest daughter in her family and has two more siblings: a brother who is 23 and a sister who is 17.

Chow did her high school at Hammond School in Columbia, SC, and is currently attending Columbia University in New York City.

Journey of her acting career

Kesley started her acting career at a very young age. In fact, she was just 13 when she played her first role as Gigi Silveri in 2003, a recurring role in One Tree Hill. She played the character until 2009.

She then guests starred on a Disney show called the Suit life of Zack and Cody in 2008. In the same year, she also got an opportunity to co-star in a Disney channel original film, Den Brother.

Disney provided her with the platform and she grasped it really well. She co-starred as Mikayla on the Disney XD original series named Pair of Kings, from 2010 to 2013. She was slowly climbing the stairs to her success.

In 2012, Kesley had a small part in The Amazing Spiderman and later landed a role in the feature film Run.

In 2014, she was cast in the Fox drama Hieroglyph but sadly the series was canceled before it premiered.

Chow had already made her name as an actress and in early 2015 she guest starred in The Goldbergs along with David Schwimmer. Apart from the TV series, Chow has also appeared in a music video for Hayley Kiyoko’s song Girls like Girls.

In 2016 Chow landed a role in a star-studded movie named Wind River where she will be playing Natalie alongside Jeremy Renner and Elizabeth Olsen.
