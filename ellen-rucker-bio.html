Early Life, Family & Education

Ellen Rucker was born in Lancaster, South Carolina, on April 27, 1977, to supporting parents Douglas Rucker and Ruby Rucker. Her father, Douglas, is a professional doctor, while Ruby is a typical housewife.

now we know where Ellen got her motivation to become a doctor.

Besides, the 46 year old grew up with her two siblings, Ruby Rucker-Cooper and Rucker-Jamison. 

Ellen attended Lancaster High School and later graduated from the University of North Carolina with a degree in medicinal studies, where she was a cheerleader for the college team. 

Rucker then attended Life University in Atlanta, studying Chiropractic medicine. 

Career

Ellen is a chiropractic medicine expert and an entrepreneur. She stayed in this profession for 11 years, became an entrepreneur, and launched her product under the brand name “Rucker Roots.”

Since then, the product has grown steadily and is one of the premier products made purely from nature.

When asked about her brand, Ellen said, “My role in the business is everything. I facilitate the shipments going out, and I’m the one who likes to do the business part of it. I talk to the chemists and the lawyers, and I enjoy that part.”

Ellen has established herself as the most popular female entrepreneur whose business solely focuses on mix-up kitchen ingredients for hair regimen. 

Other than that, the South Carolina native also appeared in a TV series called “Love Thy Sister” in 2015.
