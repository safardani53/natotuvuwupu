THE nation's biggest bookstore chain has confirmed all locations will close for the whole day later this month.

It follows a similar announcement by retailers Target and Walmart.

Barnes and Noble will close all stores on Christmas Day, December 25.

The book retailer has approximately 600 stores in all 50 states.

However, the closure will be just for one day only.

Barnes and Noble stores will be open as normal on Christmas Eve - good news for last-minute shoppers.
