Career

  • O’Keefe made his television debut in 2003 on Channel Seven’s Big Bite. Later that year, he started presenting Deal or No Deal.
  • In 2005, O’Keefe co-hosted the tri-network tsunami relief appeal Reach Out, which raised funds for recovery operations around Asia. In the same year, he co-presented the 47th TV Week Logie Awards and hosted the short-lived Dragons Den.
  • O’Keefe co-hosted Weekend Sunrise on Saturday and Sunday mornings with journalist Monique Wright from 2005 to 2017.
  • He also filled in for David Koch on Sunrise on a regular basis. During the summer of 2013, he co-hosted Weekend Sunrise with his previous Weekend Sunrise co-host Samantha Armytage for two weeks while Koch was on vacation.
  • O’Keefe is a former chairperson of Australia’s White Ribbon Campaign, an organization committed to the prevention of violence against women. He was one of the campaign’s original members in Australia and has served as an ambassador since 2004.
